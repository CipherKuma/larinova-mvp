Larinova Onboarding Flow — Step Overview

5-step flow that builds to the "aha moment" — speaking Tamil and seeing SOAP notes appear

1

"What brings you here?"

Motivation cards — 3 selectable problem statements. Full-screen animated gradient. Establishes mental model before any form-filling. Doctor feels seen.

  • "I spend too much time writing notes after consultations"
  • "I want to document in Tamil but type in English"
  • "I need proper Indian prescription formats"
2

"Tell us about you"

Compact profile step — specialty selection (visual cards) + registration details (degrees, council, reg number). All merged into one step. Clinic details collected later in settings. Only specialty is required.

3

⭐ "Try the magic" — THE Aha Moment

Live transcription demo. Doctor taps mic, speaks Tamil/English for 5-10 seconds. Sarvam STT transcribes live. Then shimmer animation → mock SOAP note generates from their words. "This is what every consultation will look like."

This is the Whispr Flow-inspired step that hooks the doctor.

4

"Your prescriptions will look like this"

Prescription preview — MCI-format PDF preview with THEIR name, degrees, and registration pre-filled from step 2. Slides up from bottom. Quick visual payoff showing the output they'll get.

5

"You're ready, Dr. [Name]"

Celebration + launch. Emerald particle burst. Welcome message with their name/specialty. Three quick-start cards pulse in: "Start consultation", "Add patient", "Explore Helena AI". Auto-redirect after 3s.

Design System

Background: Video + gradient overlay (same as auth pages) across all steps
Progress: Animated emerald line at top (not dots)
Transitions: Full-screen crossfade with scale
Micro-interactions: Every selection triggers emerald pulse glow
Typography: Outfit headings, word-by-word entrance animation
