4. Certificate Control Points

Control pointWhy it matters
Authenticated doctorPrevents anonymous certificate generation.
Patient linked to doctor recordKeeps certificate tied to real OPD workflow.
Doctor registration numberRequired trust signal for Indian certificates.
Patient signature/thumb and identification markRequired in the recommended Indian certificate format.
Draft status before signaturePrevents AI/document draft from being treated as final.
Doctor signature and clinic sealRequired before external use.
Document record copySupports register/copy retention.
Future QR/verification linkHelps hospitals, HR, and recipients verify authenticity.
