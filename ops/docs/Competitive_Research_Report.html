Section 6

Strategic Recommendations

1. Lead with language, not features. "The only AI scribe that speaks Indonesian" is infinitely more compelling than a feature list. Every competitor has SOAP notes and ICD-10 codes. Nobody else has native Bahasa Indonesia.

2. Your price is a weapon, not a weakness. At Rp 149K/month you're cheaper than clinic management tools that don't even generate notes. Frame it as: "Less than the cost of one patient consultation."

3. Don't build clinic management features yet. Klinik Pintar, Assist.id, and Trustmedis already own scheduling, billing, and staff management. Compete on what they can't do — AI note generation. Integrate with them later rather than rebuilding what they've already built.

4. Watch Medow Health AI closely. They're the only company claiming Bahasa Indonesia scribe support. If they enter Indonesia, your advantages are price (likely 3–5x cheaper) and local-first design.

5. Future integration play: Build toward SatuSehat and BPJS integration. This becomes a massive moat — global competitors will never prioritize Indonesian regulatory compliance.
