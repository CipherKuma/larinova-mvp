3. Sensitivity — what survives delay

If Indonesia is delayed by 12 months

No impact

India motion delivers all committed milestones independently. ₹8 L allocated to Indonesia rolls into a 4-month runway extension.

If first hire is delayed by 3 months

+1.5 month runway

₹2 L returned to founder runway pool. Operational risk: founder bus-factor extends; net-positive on cash, net-negative on resilience.

If hospital pipeline closes 30% slower

~₹15 L ARR shortfall

Year-1 ARR target lands ~₹35 L instead of ₹50 L. Series Seed raise compresses to ₹15–30 Cr post-money but still proceeds.

If AI inference cost doubles

Margin: 85% → 70%

Still elite SaaS unit economics. Mitigated by Claude Service caching layer, free-tier 20-consult cap, model-swap flexibility.

4. Monthly burn profile — blended steady-state

Once the first strategic hire onboards (month 9), the monthly burn settles at approximately ₹3.4 Lakh per month blended across founder runway, hire salary, infra, BD, and tools. The runway calculation assumes no revenue offset — every Pro doctor that converts effectively extends runway beyond 22 months. At 100 paying doctors (~₹1 L MRR), the company is cash-flow neutral relative to ongoing burn.

Bottom line for the investor: ₹75 L produces 22 months of runway in the worst case (zero revenue), 28+ months in the realistic case (modest revenue offset from month 4 onwards), and 36+ months in the upside case (Indonesia traction). At month 12 the company is either Series Seed-able (₹50 L ARR achieved) or cash-flow-positive on a smaller doctor base. Either path returns Sundar's cheque at a 5–10× multiple within 18–24 months from today.
